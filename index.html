Documentation

Firstly, I was inspired by the upcoming Halloween holiday. However, I'm not big on horror; in fact, spiders are about as much scare as I can take, so I decided to make a game with a more cute Halloween vibe.

I also decided to lean into the idea of trick OR treat. Whenever we think about it, we always forget the trick aspect of the trick or treat. I thought, wouldn't it be fun to make a game where you can go virtual trick-or-treating (especially since no one can go trick or treating in person this year) that incorporates the "trick" into the game?

Also I thought the concept of an adorable grave-robbing ghost was pretty funny.

Trick or Treat

Our ghost friend Casper is looking to trick or treat this halloween!

Hit the key 1, 2, or 3 depending on how hard you want the game to be!

How to Play

Use the arrow keys to navigate around the graveyard, and 'a' to unlock the trick or treat within each headstone.

Watch out! If it's a trick, you'll have to run away until the trick goes off the screen.
